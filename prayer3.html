Personalized Prayer for Me to Follow God’s Guidance

Heavenly Father (Avinu Shebashamayim),

Thank You for the gift of salvation through Jesus (Yeshua) Christ. I come to You asking for Your strength and guidance as I seek to follow Your will:

  • Lead me by the Holy Spirit (Ruach HaKodesh) and help me to live in a way that honors You, seeking to walk according to Your purpose without the need for correction.
  • Empower me to embrace Your discipline as an opportunity to grow and keep my heart open to Your conviction.
  • Transform my desires, Father (Avinu), that they may align with Your purpose:
    • Give me spiritual eyes to see life as You do, and a heart that hungers for Your Word.
    • Surround me with fellowship and accountability, so I am encouraged and supported in my journey with You.
    • Guide me toward spiritual maturity, and may my decisions always reflect Your truth.
    • May I live each day more fully surrendered to You, trusting in Your guidance and growing in the grace You’ve given me.

In Jesus’ (Yeshua’s) name, Amen.

Holy Scripture References:

  • Hebrews 12:7 – "If you endure chastening, God deals with you as with sons; for what son is there whom a father does not chasten?"
  • 2 Corinthians 5:17 – "Therefore, if anyone is in Christ, he is a new creation; old things have passed away; behold, all things have become new."
  • John 10:27 – "My sheep hear My voice, and I know them, and they follow Me."
  • Psalm 37:4 – "Delight yourself also in the Lord, and He shall give you the desires of your heart."
