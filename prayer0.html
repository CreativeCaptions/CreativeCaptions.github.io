The Incense of Prayer — Process & Flow

A simple, step-by-step presentation of how prayers move from the saint’s cry to God’s response.

  1. 1. You Pray

    Actor: You (the saint)

    Action: Raw cry, need, or intercession rises.

    “The Spirit helps us in our weakness… intercedes with groanings.” — Romans 8:26

  2. 2. Yeshua Receives & Sanctifies

    Actor: Jesus (High Priest)

    Action: Adds His incense (blood, merit, righteousness) — makes the prayer acceptable.

    “Another angel with a golden censer… was given much incense to offer with the prayers.” — Revelation 8:3

  3. 3. Angel Places on Altar

    Actor: Angel (ministering spirit)

    Action: Pours prayer + incense onto the golden altar before the throne.

    “The smoke of the incense, with the prayers, rose before God from the angel’s hand.” — Revelation 8:4

  4. 4. God Decides & Releases

    Actor: God the Father

    Action: Processes the prayer according to His will and adds fire (power).

    “The angel took the censer, filled it with fire from the altar, and threw it.” — Revelation 8:5

  5. 5. Judgment / Answer Falls

    Actor: Holy Spirit / Angels

    Action: Fire, hail, earthquake, healing, revival — whatever fits God’s purpose.

    “There were peals of thunder… and 100-lb hail.” — Revelation 8:5; 16:21

Key Clarifications

  • Does Jesus place it on the altar? No — an angel does. Jesus prepares it.
  • Is the angel Jesus? No. Revelation distinguishes “another angel” from “the Lamb.”
  • Does God “process” it? Yes — He sovereignly decides when and how to answer.
  • Is every prayer answered with hail? No. Answers vary: healing, revival, protection, or quiet grace.

Flow:
