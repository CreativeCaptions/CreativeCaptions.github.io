The Heavenly Prayer Pipeline: Who Does What, Step by Step  

1. You Pray
Actor: You (the saint)
Action: Raw cry, need, or intercession rises.
Scripture: “The Spirit helps us in our weakness… intercedes with groanings.” (Romans 8:26)

2. Yeshua Receives & Sanctifies
Actor: Jesus (High Priest)
Action: Adds His incense (blood, merit, righteousness) → makes prayer acceptable.
Scripture: “Another angel with a golden censer… was given much incense to offer with the prayers.” (Revelation 8:3)

3. Angel Places on Altar
Actor: Angel (ministering spirit)
Action: Pours prayer + incense onto golden altar before the throne.
Scripture: “The smoke of the incense, with the prayers, rose before God from the angel’s hand.” (Revelation 8:4)

4. God Decides & Releases
Actor: God the Father
Action: Processes the prayer according to His will → adds fire (power).
Scripture: “The angel took the censer, filled it with fire from the altar, and threw it.” (Revelation 8:5)

5. Judgment / Answer Falls
Actor: Holy Spirit / Angels
Action: Fire, hail, earthquake, healing, revival — whatever fits God’s purpose.
Scripture: “There were peals of thunder… and 100-lb hail.” (Revelation 8:5; 16:21)

Key Clarifications
Does Jesus place it on the altar? No — an angel does. Jesus prepares it; the angel presents it.
Is the angel Jesus? No. Revelation distinguishes “another angel” from “the Lamb.”
Does God “process” it? Yes — He sovereignly decides when and how to answer.
Is every prayer answered with hail? No. Some become healing, revival, protection, or silent grace.

Visual Flow
Your Prayer (“Help!”) → Yeshua (Adds Incense – His Blood) → Angel (Pours onto Golden Altar) → God the Father (Adds Fire – His Will) → Thrown to Earth → Answer
