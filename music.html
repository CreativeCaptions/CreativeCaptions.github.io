Learning Path with Free Apps

  1. Perfect Ear (Start Here)
    Purpose: Build your ear, understand music theory basics.
    • Learn notes, intervals, scales, chords, rhythm
    • Use the Guided Courses (e.g., "Music Theory Basics" and "Ear Training Basics")
    • Practice daily for 10–15 min
    • Think of this as your music school + ear gym
  2. Autochords (Step Two)
    Purpose: Learn and hear real-world chord progressions used in songs.
    • Pick a mood or key (e.g., “Happy” in C major)
    • See what progressions work (like C–G–Am–F)
    • Listen to how chords sound together
    • This builds your songwriting intuition
  3. Piano Companion: Chords, Scales (Step Three)
    Purpose: Study individual chords, scales, and music structure.
    • Use to understand what’s inside each chord or scale
    • Explore inversions, formulas, and keys
    • Use the Circle of Fifths to see which chords work in a key
    • This is your reference manual while songwriting
  4. GarageBand (Final Step)
    Purpose: Create your own music using what you've learned.
    • Use Smart Piano/Guitar to build your chord progression
    • Record vocals or melodies over it
    • Experiment with drums, bass, loops
    • Think of this as your recording studio
