Personalized Prayer for Me to Follow God’s Guidance

Heavenly Father (Avinu Shebashamayim),

  • Thank You for the gift of salvation through Jesus (Yeshua) Christ.
  • I come asking for:
    • Your strength and guidance as I seek to follow Your will.
    • Leading by the Holy Spirit (Ruach HaKodesh) to live in a way that honors You.
  • Empower me to:
    • Walk according to Your purpose without the need for correction.
    • Embrace Your discipline as an opportunity to grow, keeping my heart open to Your conviction.
  • Transform my desires, Father (Avinu), to align with Your purpose.
  • Grant me:
    • Spiritual eyes to see life as You do.
    • A heart that hungers for Your Word.
  • Surround me with:
    • Fellowship and accountability, for encouragement and support on my journey with You.
  • Guide me toward spiritual maturity, and may my decisions always reflect Your truth.
  • May I live each day more fully surrendered to You, trusting in Your guidance and growing in Your grace.

In Jesus' (Yeshua’s) name, Amen.
