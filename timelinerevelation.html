The Timeline of the Book of Revelation

PRE-TRIBULATION

The Seven Seals (Revelation 6:1–8:1)

  • The First Seal: The White Horse (6:1-2)
  • The Second Seal: The Red Horse (6:3-4)
  • The Third Seal: The Black Horse (6:5-6)
  • The Fourth Seal: The Pale Horse (6:7-8)
  • The Fifth Seal: The Cry of the Martyrs (6:9-11)
  • The Sixth Seal: Cosmic Disturbances (6:12-17)

INTERLUDE BEGINS

  • The Sealing of the 144,000 (7:1-8)
  • The Great Multitude in White Robes (7:9-17) – Rapture of the Church

INTERLUDE ENDS

The Seventh Seal: Silence in Heaven (8:1)

The Seven Trumpets (Revelation 8:2–11:19)

  • The First Trumpet: Hail and Fire Mixed with Blood (8:6-7)
  • The Second Trumpet: A Mountain Thrown into the Sea (8:8-9)
  • The Third Trumpet: The Star Wormwood (8:10-11)
  • The Fourth Trumpet: The Sun, Moon, and Stars Struck (8:12)
  • The Three Woes Begin (8:13)
  • The Fifth Trumpet: The First Woe—The Plague of Locusts (9:1-12) – Lasts 150 days

TRIBULATION BEGINS (2 x 42 months)

  • The Sixth Trumpet: The Second Woe—The Release of the Four Angels (9:13-21)
  • The Angel and the Little Scroll (10:1-11)
  • The Seven Thunders (10:3-4)
  • The Two Witnesses (11:1-14)
  • The Second Woe Ends (11:14)

THE GREAT TRIBULATION BEGINS (42 Months)

  • The Seventh Trumpet: The Third Woe—The Kingdom Proclaimed (11:15-19)
  • The Woman, the Child, and the Dragon (Revelation 12:1-17)
  • The Woman and the Dragon (12:1-6)
  • War in Heaven (12:7-12)
  • The Dragon Pursues the Woman (12:13-17)
  • The Two Beasts (Revelation 13:1-18)
  • The Beast from the Sea (13:1-10)
  • The Beast from the Earth (13:11-18)
  • The Lamb and the 144,000 (Revelation 14:1-5)
  • The Three Angels’ Messages (Revelation 14:6-13)
  • The Harvest of the Earth (Revelation 14:14-20)

The Seven Bowls (Plagues) (Revelation 15:1–16:21)

  • Preparation for the Bowls (15:1-8)
  • The First Bowl: Painful Sores (16:1-2)
  • The Second Bowl: The Sea Turns to Blood (16:3)
  • The Third Bowl: Rivers and Springs Become Blood (16:4-7)
  • The Fourth Bowl: Scorching Heat (16:8-9)
  • The Fifth Bowl: Darkness and Pain (16:10-11)
  • The Sixth Bowl: The Euphrates Dried Up (16:12-16)
  • The Seventh Bowl: Earthquake and Hailstorm (16:17-21)

The Fall of Babylon the Great (Revelation 17:1–19:10)

  • The Prostitute on the Beast (17:1-18)
  • The Fall of Babylon (18:1-24)
  • Rejoicing in Heaven (19:1-10)
  • The Rider on the White Horse and the Defeat of the Beast (Revelation 19:11-21)

THE TRIBULATION AND GREAT TRIBULATION ENDS

  • The Thousand Years (Revelation 20:1-6)
  • The Defeat of Satan (Revelation 20:7-10)
  • The Judgment Before the Great White Throne (Revelation 20:11-15)
  • A New Heaven and a New Earth (Revelation 21:1-8)
  • The New Jerusalem (Revelation 21:9-27)
  • The River of Life (Revelation 22:1-5)
  • Jesus Is Coming Soon (Revelation 22:6-21)
